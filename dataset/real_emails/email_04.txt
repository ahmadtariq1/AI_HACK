Subject: You’re In! Qualification confirmed - ICPC Asia Topi Regional Onsite Contest 2025

Body: Dear Team BruteForce,
Warm greetings from ICPC Asia Topi!

We are delighted to inform you that your team has been successfully shortlisted from “The 2025 ICPC Asia Topi Online Preliminary Programming Contest” to participate in the upcoming "The 2025 ICPC Asia Topi Regional Onsite Contest." Congratulations on this remarkable achievement!

The Regional Onsite Contest is scheduled for 31st January and 1st February 2026. We are excited to host you and your team for this prestigious event, where you can showcase your exceptional programming skills and compete against some of the finest talents from across Pakistan. The top 106 teams who had solved a minimum of three problems in the online contest have been shortlisted.

To ensure your participation in the Regional Onsite Contest, please take note of the following important details:

1) Registration Process:

·         Registration opens on 16th December 2025 and closes on 15th January 2026.

·         The registration fee is 45,000 PKR per team.

·         The registration fee for coaches is 5000 PKR per night in the guest house or 2000 PKR in the hostel on a shared basis.
